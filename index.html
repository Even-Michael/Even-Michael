History of DC Speedsters

The Speed Force is an energy field that grants all speedsters their power. Barry Allen generates the Speed Force. Several speedsters have merged with it, including Barry Allen, Johnny Quick, and Max Mercury. Scientists on the planet Savoth had a deep understanding of the Speed Force and there were many universities devoted to its study.
